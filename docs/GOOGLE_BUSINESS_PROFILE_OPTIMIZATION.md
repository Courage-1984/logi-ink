## Reviews & Reputation

### Getting Your First Reviews

**Strategies:**
1. **Ask Satisfied Clients:** Reach out to past clients via email or phone
2. **Make It Easy:** Provide a direct link to your Google Business Profile review page
3. **Follow Up:** Send a follow-up email after project completion
4. **Incentivize (Ethically):** Offer a small thank-you (not payment for reviews)

### Review Link

**Share this link with clients:**
```
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID
```

**Or use this simpler method:**
1. Go to your Google Business Profile
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy the short link provided
4. Share with clients via email, SMS, or on your website

### Responding to Reviews

**Best Practices:**
- Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Be professional and courteous
- Thank reviewers for their feedback
- Address concerns in negative reviews
- Keep responses concise (2-3 sentences)

**Response Templates:**

**Positive Review Response:**
```
Thank you so much for your kind words, [Name]! We're thrilled that you're happy with [specific service]. It was a pleasure working with you, and we look forward to serving you again in the future.
```

**Negative Review Response:**
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're sorry to hear about your experience. We'd like to make this right. Please contact us directly at info@logi-ink.co.za or +27 79 552 3726 so we can address your concerns.
```

**Action Items:**
1. Set up review request email template
2. Send to 5-10 past clients this week
3. Monitor reviews daily
4. Respond to all reviews within 48 hours

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Profile Not Showing in Search:**
- Ensure profile is verified
- Complete all required information
- Add photos and posts
- Wait 1-2 weeks for indexing

**Low Visibility:**
- Improve description with keywords
- Get more reviews
- Post regularly
- Add more photos

**Negative Reviews:**
- Respond professionally
- Address concerns directly
- Offer to resolve offline
- Don't delete (can't delete Google reviews)

**Duplicate Listings:**
- Claim and verify all listings
- Mark duplicates as "duplicate" in Google Business Profile
- Request removal of duplicates
